Our Story

Founded on Faith &
Academic Excellence

Devine Secondary School is a private O and A-Level, mixed, day and boarding school located along Entebbe Road via Broadway–Kitala. Founded on 19th February 2004 by Mr. and Mrs. Tabaro, the school was built on Christian values to empower the next generation.

Nationally registered with UNEB Centre No. U2911, we offer the new Ugandan lower secondary curriculum and the British curriculum at A-Level, preparing students for university, career, and life in service to God and community.

Admissions 2025/26

Applications are now open. Secure your child's place at Devine Secondary School, Kitala–Entebbe.

1

Submit Application

Complete the online form or visit the school at Kitala–Broadway, Entebbe Road. No application fee required.

2

Provide Required Documents

P.L.E results (S.1 entry), O-Level results (S.5 entry), birth certificate, passport photos, and most recent school reports.

3

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted applicants sit a brief entrance test in English and Mathematics. Results released within 5 working days.

4

Offer & Acceptance

Successful applicants receive an offer letter. Confirmed upon payment of registration fee and submission of fee structure documents.

5

Welcome to Devine!

Orientation day held before term. Parents meet teachers, collect timetables, school uniform requirements and term calendar.
