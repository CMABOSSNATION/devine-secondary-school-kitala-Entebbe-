Faith & Academic Excellence

Our environment is safe, inclusive, and inspiring — guided by Christian values that shape students into responsible, compassionate leaders ready to serve their communities and the world.

Why Choose Us

The Devine Difference

We invest in every student — academically, spiritually, physically, and socially.

01

Qualified, Dedicated Teachers

All our teachers are degree-qualified and undergo continuous professional development. Average teaching experience: 14 years.

02

Modern Infrastructure

Fully-equipped science labs, a 60-PC computer lab, a well-stocked library, two sports fields, and an assembly hall with 800-seat capacity.

03

Proven ZIMSEC Results

Consistent top-3 ranking in the district for both O-Level and A-Level results. 98% pass rate in the 2024 examinations.

04

Safe Boarding Facilities

Separate male and female boarding houses, 24-hour security, qualified matrons, and a fully-staffed sick bay with a resident nurse.

05

Counselling & Support

Professional guidance counsellors provide academic, career, and personal counselling. Peer mentorship programme for all students.

06

Parent Portal Access

Parents access real-time results, attendance records, fee statements, and school communications via our online portal and WhatsApp updates.

Admissions 2025/26

Applications are open. Secure your child's place at Devine Secondary School.

1

Submit Application Form

Complete the online form or collect a physical form from the school office. No application fees.

2

Provide Required Documents

Birth certificate, Grade 7 results (for Form 1), O-Level results (for 6th Form), passport photos, and previous school reports.

3

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted applicants sit a brief entrance assessment in English and Mathematics. Results released within 5 days.

4

Offer Letter & Acceptance

Successful applicants receive an offer letter. Acceptance confirmed upon payment of registration fee.

5

Welcome to Devine!

Orientation day held before term begins. Parents meet teachers, collect timetables and school materials.
